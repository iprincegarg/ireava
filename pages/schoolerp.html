What Is A School Management Software?

School management software is an information management and ERP system used by educational institutions to manage all student and staff data, transactions, and other modules records. A school management software is designed to manage & simplify the day-to-day administrative & non-administrative tasks of a school. School management software enables schools to monitor daily activities like daily attendance, timetable management, etc. from a single centralized platform. From managing online admissions, assigning homework, online exams, examination management, finance and payroll management, and library management, etc. The school ERP software offers various modules for complete single and multi-campus administrations & enables educators to make quick & thoughtful decisions that enhance student & faculty performance & drive institutional growth.
