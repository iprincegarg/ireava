Leave management

Ireava leave management system helps you to manage staff and students leave and mark leave in the attendance automatically if accepted.

Leave management


  • Students can apply for leave from their app.
  • Staff can apply leave from their portal.
  • Admin can accept or reject leave with a reason.
  • Automatically leave will be marked in the attendance if accepted.
  • Staff salary will be deducted based on the attendance.
  • Get pending leaves alert on the dashboard.
