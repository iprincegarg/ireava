Reception Desk

Manage visitor's and their queries and admission forms and process of reception.

Reception Desk Features


  • Manage admission queries, leads, follow up dates and history.
  • Manage visitors record, give then slip or RFID to manage In and Out time.
  • Manage phone logs to manage follow up time for admission and other queries.
  • Generate gate pass for the students' who are going home in the midst of the classes and half day will be marked automatically for the students.
  • Get gate pass details studentswise, pickup personwise, daily report and so on.
  • Manage complaints raised by visitors, parents, students or any staff.
  • Manage complaints status & priority.
  • Assign complaints to the departments & maintain update log.
  • Manage postal dispatched or received records.
  • Manage staff resumes and applied staff details for the job with the shortlisted option.
  • Setup front desk inside menu dynamically as you want.
