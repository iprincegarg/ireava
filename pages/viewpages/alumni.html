Alumni Management

Ireava provides complete alumni management solution to manage passed or left students and invite them for the event.

Alumni Module Features


  • Manage school passed or left students.
  • Manage their current details to contact them.
  • Manage events for the alumni students.
  • Contact them for event via mail and phone sms.
  • Get class wise and year wise alumni students details.
