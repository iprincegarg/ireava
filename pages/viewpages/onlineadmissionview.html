Online Admission

Ireava provides online admission facility that helps schools to get the new admissions without any hassle and skip the long admission process.

Online Admission


  • Create online admission form or use pre-defined form.
  • Collect payment during online admission or skip it.
  • Get records of the students who did payment and who didn't.
  • Students will get the form of submission with reference ID.
  • Students who paid with online admission will get auto enrolment.
