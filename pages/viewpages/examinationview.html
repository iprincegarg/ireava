Examination Management

Ireava provides an examination management module which can help schools to manage exam marks for students and parents can see exam records for its children respectively on mobile application.

Examination Module Features


  • Define grading system with percentage range.
  • create exams for a whole school and an individual classes.
  • Create and generate exam schedule.
  • Enable/disable report card if fees is pending.
  • Staff, parents and students can check exam schedule on their panel and mobile application.
  • Register marks obtained by students in every subject.
  • Mark absent for student if absent in exam.
  • Re-schedule exam for absent students and update marks.
  • Generate Mark Sheet of the exam of every student.
  • Students' can download Mark sheet in Pdf from their application.
  • Generate exam Admit Card for the students.
  • Parents can check their student's exam result only.
  • Students can check their exam result on application or on panel.
  • Admin can export whole class exam result sheet in multiple formats.
  • Exam slip printing helps to generate slip for exam students and subjects.
  • Create online examination for the students.
  • Provide instant online exam result or publish on any date.
  • Generate marks report card or grading report card.
  • Show multiple subjects average or sum into one subject.
  • Show extra curriculum, extra skills, attendance obtained and other fields.
  • Subject bifurcation into multiple fields.
  • Show subjects marks graph on report card.
  • Show students comparative analysis on report card.
  • Show upto top 10 students on report card.
