Communication Management

Ireava communication module allows a seamless engagement with staff, parents and students.

Communication Module Features


  • Send email / SMS to any staff, parents and students in a group, individual or a whole class.
  • Send important notices to the staff, parents and students.
  • Track history of send notices, emails and SMS.
  • Auto SMS of attendance, login credentials, admission done, homework assigned, fee submit, fee due dates, birthday, exam results to parents and students.
  • Real time communication and alert via push notifications.
