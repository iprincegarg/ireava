Academics Management

Ireava provides complete academics management solution to manage classes, sections, timetables, promote students and so on.

Academics Module Features


  • Manage school sections, classes.
  • Manage subjects for a class, assign subjects to the teachers.
  • Assign class teacher so that every class has a incharge to manage and mark attendance.
  • Create time table for each class easily.
  • Manage every student additional elective subjects.
  • Students can take classes, give exams as per their additional elective subjects also.
  • Print time table for classes.
  • Change substitute teacher for absent teachers.
  • Promote students to the other class, transfer in the another section of the same class.
  • Manage school scholars and show them on website and application.
  • Update whole class old roll number to the new roll number at a time.
  • Switch whole section students from one section to other in same class.
