HR Management

Ireava provides a complete HR Management system. Manage payroll, attendance, hire new staff and more features.

Human Resource Module Features


  • Manage department, designation and permissions for staff.
  • Hire new staff, define leaves, payroll for them.
  • Get 360 deg view of staff profile.
  • Send auto login credentials to staff email address whenever hired new one.
  • Manage staff documents at one place.
  • Generate payroll slip and pay record of staff salary.
  • Pay salary to staff and keep track record of every month salary.
  • Automatic deduction of staff salary as per the staff attendance.
  • Add extra earnings and deduction in the individual staff payroll.
  • Automatic calculation of salary using the earnings and deduction parameters defined once in the individual staff payroll.
  • Students can provide rating to the teacher based on their behaviour and teaching experience.
  • Accept or reject leaves and access leave record of staffs during payroll generation.
  • Provide access permission to staff for their work profile.
  • Enable/Disable staff login for the security purpose.
  • Check login activities for staff members.
  • View staff activities log detail.
  • Manage staff timeline about their work and achievements.
