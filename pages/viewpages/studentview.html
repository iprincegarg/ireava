Student Management

Ireava provides you a complete student management system. Add new students, track students record, manage their activities and a lot more features.

Student Information Module Features


  • Manage students, guardians, siblings and other details at one place.
  • Admit new students with 40+ fields in admission form.
  • Add custom fields in the student form to make it flexible as per school use.
  • Import students using csv at first time to save time and manual effort.
  • Auto generation of login credentials for students and parents.
  • Manage student documents at one place.
  • Get 360 deg view of a student profile to get more information at one place.
  • Coaching institutes can add student in multiple classes using single admission number.
  • Get student history record from the starting of the admission.
  • Export student details in csv, excel and pdf format.
  • Send login credentials to parents and students on their number or email them for login on school app.
  • Enable/Disable student login into the system or application.
  • Get class subject report of every class.
  • Check login activities for students and parents.
  • Manage student's timeline regarding their achievements, health report etc.
