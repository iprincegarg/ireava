Fees Management

Ireava provides the more powerful fee management system where you can manage everything about fee records, slips and check records anytime.

Fees Module Features


  • Collect students fee and check complete details of previous fee record.
  • Collect students fee in bulk.
  • Generate custom fees receipt for parents.
  • Manage Due fees of students' class wise and fees type wise.
  • Enable/Disable report card if fees is pending.
  • Search fees payment information by payment Id.
  • Search individual student fees record.
  • Check balance fees report of a whole class.
  • Day book of a school.
  • Check balance fees report of a student.
  • Check balance fees report of all classes and individual class.
  • Generate balance fees slip for the students.
  • Assign different fees type to a whole class or individual student.
  • Auto assign fee groups to the new or old students.
  • Get the analysed data graphs to understand data easily.
  • sibling's details on collect fee portal to collect siblings fee quickly.
  • Auto suggestion list of students by name, admission number, phone number and using more variables.
  • Auto Send email, SMS, application notification to parents on fees submission.
  • Take fees online from parent's using payment gateway.
  • Assign custom discount groups, fine to individual students.
  • Get list of student's before and after due date to remind them with auto SMS and email send function.
  • Print and generate fee slip for parents.
  • Students' and parents' can download fees slip pdf from their application.
  • Set customized fees receipt format.
  • Create multiple fees books for different types of fees.
  • Collect fees using one of the multiple fees books.
  • Export data in multiple formats like: csv, excel, pdf etc.
  • Import old fee master structure for new session.
  • Import fees structure using csv format.
